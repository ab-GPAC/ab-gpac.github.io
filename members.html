Members

The Alberta Graduate Provincial Advocacy Council (ab-GPAC) is composed of four member organizations:

 

Athabasca University Graduate Students’ Association (AUGSA),  the University of Albera (U of A) GSA, the University of Calgary (U of C) GSA and the University of Lethbridge (U of L) GSA.

These four universities are designated as Comprehensive Academic and Research Institutions (CARI) in the Post-Secondary Learning Act.

Each institution appoints three representatives that sit on ab-GPAC’s Board of Directors.
